# Changelog

## 0.1.0

Initial repo-ready research package.

Includes:

- 3-prime Beatty/floor-sum ranker;
- 5-prime layer-compressed MITM kernel;
- sums-only adaptive MITM fallback;
- independent interval-rank auditor for `k <= 6`, primes subset `{2,3,5,7,11,13}`;
- standard DP/pointer and Cartesian-sum comparator workbenches;
- smoke tests and paper skeleton.
